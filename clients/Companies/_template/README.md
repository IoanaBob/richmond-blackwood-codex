## Record Routing

Long-lived operational records should live in the matching Notion table and link back to the company when the schema supports it. This includes company information, contracts, employment, payroll/payslips, invoices, expenses, correspondence, tasks, and filing records.

Drive should hold source documents and records that do not fit cleanly into Notion or do not need frequent operational access. In git, keep pointers and routing notes only.

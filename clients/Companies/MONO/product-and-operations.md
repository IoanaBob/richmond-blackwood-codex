## Operational Requirements

- Customer uploads exactly 30 photos and required text fields.
- Customer pays at checkout.
- MONO designers create/review a PDF draft.
- Customer approves or requests changes under a defined revision policy.
- Approved print-ready file is sent to Gelato.
- Customer receives tracking.
- Photos/drafts are retained only as needed for fulfillment/support and then deleted under the approved policy.

## Privacy And Rights Boundary

The project requires terms allowing MONO and necessary third-party processors to process uploaded photos only to create, review, print, ship, and support the ordered book.

Customer photos must not be sold, reused for unrelated websites/products, used for model training, or used in marketing without direct customer approval.

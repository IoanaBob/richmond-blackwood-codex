## Strategic Evolution

The new marketing model should include Authority Calling / Authority Liaison as a service and lead wedge.

Detailed product/service and pricing context:

- `internal/product-offerings.md`

Source docs:

- Authority Calling Product Strategy: `https://www.notion.so/34ce41301314810caa51c4eb82ca34f0`
- Authority Liaison Bot project: `https://www.notion.so/34ce4130131480adbf76f90e5fad640f`

Provisional positioning:

- Customers submit authority issues in English.
- RB contacts authorities/offices where possible, including in relevant languages where supported.
- RB returns a written English outcome summary.
- The service creates paid intent and structured problem data.
- High-fit follow-ups can convert into tax, VAT, payroll, company administration, company structuring, freelancer setup, cross-border tax, and ongoing financial management work.

## Claim Review Queue

The old proof-point question is too broad. Review should happen claim by claim:

- Is the EUR 10,500 yearly average savings claim approved and current?
- Is the "EUR 80k income/revenue" threshold approved for targeting?
- Is the "lower tax by up to 14%" claim approved and substantiated?
- Can RB say "full service" and "no hidden costs" if Authority Calling includes possible POA surcharge and other add-ons?
- Can RB say it protects liability, or should that be softened to operational risk/admin risk?
- Can investment compounding examples remain in ads, or do they distract from Authority Calling?

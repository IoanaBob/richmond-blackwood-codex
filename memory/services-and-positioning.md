## Provisional Positioning

RB appears positioned around high-trust, cross-border company services for high earners and founders. The service package is broader than one-off company formation; it includes continuing company management and administrative execution.

Provisional service categories found in local memory and code:

- Residency and immigration support.
- Company formation and structuring.
- Wealth and tax management.
- Banking setup.
- Registered office and company secretary support.
- Cross-border planning.
- Employer-of-record or contractor hosting.
- Accounting, bookkeeping, payroll, VAT, annual returns, and filings.
- Authority calling / authority liaison: contacting public authorities, tax offices, revenue bodies, health insurers, company registries, banks, and administrative offices on behalf of clients or professional firms, then returning a written English outcome summary.
